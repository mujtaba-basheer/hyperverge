Frequently Asked Questions:

Is it 100% RBI compliant?

Yes, all our products are 100% RBI Compliant. HyperVerge is ISO 27001:2013 certified and the Video KYC product has been tested for security by a CERT-In empanelled auditor.

How long is the integration process?

We can support go-live in less than a week provided everything is ready from the client's end.

What is the process for availing your services?

You can fill-out the request form and we can get you started with the 15 days trial period with signing of the NDA. Post the trial process, we will provide you with the product credentials after signing of the service agreement.

Can we do free trial / POC?

Yes, we support 15 days of free trial. There is an NDA that we sign with our clients post which we share the test credentials for the POC process.

What is the pricing structure

We use "Tiered rate model" and the pricing is based on actual API usage. The number of units purchased will be priced for the tiers it covers.

What is the method of integration?

We support both SDK & API integration.
