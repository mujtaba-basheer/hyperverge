Local Governments | Property Tax

Our Mission is to empower governments to use Artificial Intelligence to improve the lives of their citizens through easy to use, impactful solutions.

The TrueChange Solution

Use AI to automatically spot high-value and unpermitted changes, which impact the tax roll, from aerial images in a matter of a few weeks.

Fair and equitable assessment

All properties are valued accurately. Unpermitted changes not missed out. Everybody pays their fair share of taxes.

Increased Efficiency

Automated workflows and high-priority queues ensure that the entire county can be appraised in weeks.

Reduced appraisal Cost per Parcel

Desktop review enables reduced and optimized field visits thereby saving time and reducing the cost per parcel.

Net Cash Positive

All unpermitted changes are brought into the tax rolls, making it a net cash positive solution with the scope to increase subsequent budgets.

Increased Data Integrity

Higher accuracies and not missing out on properties ensures data integrity for assessment and high-quality valuation models.

Increased Staff Safety

Appraisers spend lesser time on the field, especially during covid, increasing safety and ensuring more time with their families.
