Authentication for the New Normal

Today, remote purchase/delivery of goods, education, healthcare, etc are the new normal. In these platforms digital age and ID verification is required to prevent mis-use and for regultory compliance.

Commerce and Delivery

The lockdown has pushed e-commerce apps to expand to new product categories like alcohol and restricted medicines - which need age and ID verification - to comply with regulatory requirements.

Remote Healthcase

When patients are not willing to go to clinics and hospitals they turn to tele-medicine apps, who need to capture ID of patients and prevent spoofing frauds. This is a regulatory requirement.

Online Edtech

Online classes can be kept simple. But online attendance, and online exams are easy to cheat on. Serious edtech platforms need to verify student identity repeatedly to maintain integrity of the system.
